## Expanding Your AI Workforce: Specialized Team Concepts

With your **Headless AI** architecture (Config + Personas + Tools), you have the blueprint to tackle nearly any high-cognitive-load domain. Here are four additional specialized teams you can build to further round out the "Overlord11" suite:

---

### 1. The Strategy-Competitive Intelligence Team
This team is designed to analyze market landscapes, monitor competitors, and perform SWOT analyses with a focus on business viability.

* **Market Scout (Agent):** Scans financial reports, SEC filings, and news for competitor movements.
* **Economic Analyst (Agent):** Interprets market trends, inflation impacts, and pricing models.
* **War-Gamer (Agent):** Simulates "If/Then" scenarios based on competitor actions.
* **Strategy Formatter (Agent):** Outputs Executive Slide Decks or Strategy Briefs.
* **Unique Tool:** `competitor_tracker.json` (pings specific company newsrooms).

---

### 2. The Creative-Worldbuilder Team
Tailored for game designers, novelists, or screenplay writers who need deep internal consistency for fictional universes.

* **Lore Master (Agent):** Maintains the "World Bible" and ensures historical consistency within the fiction.
* **Linguist (Agent):** Develops naming conventions or basic dialects for fictional cultures.
* **Character Architect (Agent):** Manages character arcs, psychological profiles, and interpersonal conflicts.
* **Continuity Critic (Agent):** Audits the output specifically for "plot holes" or breaks in world logic.
* **Unique Tool:** `lore_database_tool.json` (reads/writes to a long-term "World Bible" file).

---

### 3. The Personal-Finance & Portfolio Team
A team focused on aggregating financial news and personal data to provide objective, non-hallucinated financial summaries.

* **Data Aggregator (Agent):** Fetches real-time tickers and macro-economic indicators.
* **Risk Auditor (Agent):** Evaluates the volatility and downside of specific financial moves.
* **Tax Strategist (Agent):** Applies general tax code logic (non-legal advice) to potential gains/losses.
* **Budget Optimizer (Agent):** Reviews spending logs to find efficiencies.
* **Unique Tool:** `ticker_api_tool.json` (connects to Yahoo Finance or Alpha Vantage).

---

### 4. The Technical-Learning & Curriculum Team
This team transforms dense, complex documentation into personalized learning paths and "Explain Like I'm Five" (ELI5) tutorials.

* **Syllabus Architect (Agent):** Breaks a complex topic into a 10-step learning progression.
* **Analogy Specialist (Agent):** Creates metaphors to explain abstract concepts (e.g., explaining "Latent Space" using a library metaphor).
* **Quiz Master (Agent):** Generates active-recall questions and coding challenges to test the user's knowledge.
* **Resource Librarian (Agent):** Finds the best YouTube videos, papers, and GitHub repos for each step.
* **Unique Tool:** `curriculum_exporter.json` (formats the path into an interactive checklist).
